The Rules of Investigation

1

The Setup

3-8 players join. One is secretly assigned as the Murderer. Everyone else is a Detective. Only the Murderer knows who they are.

2

Investigation Rounds

Each round, every player receives personal clues — evidence only they can see. Clues gradually point toward the Murderer. The Murderer gets misleading clues they can use to deflect.

3

Share & Discuss

Share clues with the group, discuss in chat, and take notes. But beware — the Murderer can lie and fabricate fake evidence!

4

Call a Meeting

Any player can call an emergency meeting to discuss suspicions. During the meeting, someone can call a vote to accuse a player.

5

Vote & Guess

If the majority votes guilty and they're right — Detectives win! But you only get 3 wrong guesses total. Use them wisely.

6

Winning

Detectives win by correctly identifying the Murderer. Murderer wins if all 3 guesses are used up or the investigation runs out of rounds.
