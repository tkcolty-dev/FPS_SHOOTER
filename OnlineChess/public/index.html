Chess Rules

The Pieces

PieceNameMovement
KingOne square in any direction
QueenAny number of squares in any direction
RookAny number of squares horizontally or vertically
BishopAny number of squares diagonally
KnightL-shape: 2 squares + 1 square, can jump pieces
PawnForward 1 (or 2 from start), captures diagonally

Special Moves

  • Castling — Move king 2 squares toward a rook. The rook jumps to the other side. Neither piece can have moved before, and the king cannot pass through check.
  • En Passant — If a pawn moves 2 squares forward and lands beside an enemy pawn, the enemy can capture it as if it moved only 1 square. Must be done immediately.
  • Promotion — When a pawn reaches the opposite end of the board, it must be promoted to a queen, rook, bishop, or knight.

Winning the Game

  • Checkmate — The king is in check and has no legal moves to escape.
  • Resignation — A player can resign at any time.
  • Timeout — In timed games, running out of time means you lose.

Draws

  • Stalemate — The player to move has no legal moves but is not in check.
  • Agreement — Both players agree to a draw.
  • Insufficient Material — Neither side has enough pieces to checkmate.

Help

Getting Started

  • Click Play Online to find or create a game
  • Set your name by clicking the edit button on the home screen
  • Choose a time control when creating a game, or play with no time limit

Playing a Game

  • Click a piece to select it, then click a highlighted square to move
  • Legal moves are shown as dots on the board
  • Use the Chat panel to message your opponent
  • You can Offer a Draw or Resign from the Actions panel

Your Profile

  • Your stats (wins, losses, draws) are saved locally in your browser
  • Change your avatar from the Profile page using the arrow buttons
  • Your rank updates automatically based on your win rate

Settings

  • Change the board theme in Settings (Classic, Ice, Forest, Royal)
  • Toggle sound effects, coordinates, and legal move highlights
  • Reset your stats if you want a fresh start

About

XS Chess

Version 1.0

A real-time online multiplayer chess game. Play against friends or strangers from around the world with customizable time controls.

Features

  • Real-time multiplayer with WebSocket connections
  • Multiple time controls (1, 3, 5, 10, 15, 30 minutes)
  • In-game chat with your opponent
  • Draw offers and resignation
  • Move history and captured pieces tracking
  • Customizable board themes
  • Local stats tracking with rank progression

Tech Stack

  • Frontend — Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS
  • Backend — Node.js + Express
  • Real-time — Socket.IO
  • Chess Engine — chess.js

Tips for Beginners

  • Start with 1.e4 or 1.d4 — they control the center immediately
  • Develop your knights before bishops in most openings
  • Castle early to protect your king
  • Don't move the same piece twice in the opening unless necessary
  • Control the center with pawns and pieces
